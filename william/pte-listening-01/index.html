A few tips before you begin

  • Listen for short word groups instead of trying to hold every word separately.
  • Start speaking as soon as the audio finishes and keep going if one word is missing.
  • For dictation, write the main sentence first, then check small words, endings, and spelling.
  • Your first attempt is the useful one, so do not aim for perfection.
